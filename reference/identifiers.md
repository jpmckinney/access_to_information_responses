## Calgary

`<YYYY>-<B|C|F|G|P>-<XXXX>[-<ZZZ>]`

* **B:** Non-federal consult
* **C:** Correction
* **F:** Federal consult
* **G:** General
* **P:** Personal

## Edmonton

`<YYYY>-<XXXX>[-<ZZZ>]`

The optional `-ZZZ` suffix is for "continuing" requests.
